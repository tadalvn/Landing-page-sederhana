About Us

At Type 1 Project, we are dedicated to delivering premium automotive detailing services for luxury, sports, and high-performance vehicles. Founded with a passion for automotive excellence, we combine professional expertise, advanced detailing techniques, and high-quality products to ensure every vehicle receives the care it deserves. Our commitment goes beyond cleaning—we focus on restoring, protecting, and enhancing every detail to preserve your vehicle's appearance, value, and prestige. With a strong emphasis on quality, precision, and customer satisfaction, Type 1 Project strives to provide an exceptional detailing experience for clients who demand the highest standards of automotive care.

We understand that every vehicle is a valuable investment and a reflection of its owner's personality. That is why we approach every project with meticulous attention to detail, ensuring flawless results that exceed expectations. From paint correction and ceramic coating to interior restoration and premium detailing packages, our services are tailored to meet the unique needs of each client and vehicle.

Driven by passion and innovation, Type 1 Project continuously adopts the latest industry standards, technologies, and products to deliver long-lasting protection and a showroom-quality finish. Our goal is to build lasting relationships with our clients by providing trusted service, outstanding craftsmanship, and an unmatched level of care for every vehicle that enters our facility.
